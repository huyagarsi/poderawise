Renowned US rapper 2 Chainz, born Tauheed Epps, has celebrated his wedding anniversary with his wife, Kesha Ward.

The couple is marking three years of their wonderful union but have been together for more than 14 years and share three children.

As they marked their third year in marriage, Chainz went on Facebook and Instagram pages, sharing lovely photos from their big day.

The first photo was of him and Kesha standing at the alter as they said their vows while looking dapper in their wedding outfits.
