Who Is Child Actor Mystic Inscho? Meet Him On Instagram

Multi-talented child actor Mystic Inscho’s bio is not available on Wikipedia. However, here’s everything to know.

Mystic Inscho is an American child actor who has 10 acting credits to his name.

He is best known for his work in Splintered, Darkness Comes, A Handful of Rust, and more. His most recent performance was in The Mysterious Benedict Society, which was released on June 25, 2021.

Mystic attends home school and goes to Los Angeles Valley College when it’s feasible. He is also a member of the National Junior Honorary Society.

Mystic Inscho Wiki

Mystic Inscho’s fame is ever-growing but he isn’t introduced on Wikipedia yet.

However, we get to read his interesting bio covered on various sites.

Gone on a year-long tour in the US and Canada in the School of Rock Broadway National Tour, he is in the lead role of Zack Mooneyham, the guitarist.

Mystic is multi-lingual who can effortlessly speak Mandarin Chinese. He has recorded songs in different languages like Spanish, Korean, Cantonese, and Tamil.

Mystic Inscho Age And Birthday

Actor Mystic Inscho’s current age is 12 years old.

He was born on December 21, 2008, in Los Angeles, California.

Mystic was already performing on stages at 4 and started acting and giving his voice in small to big films as early as the age of 8.

He has molded himself from this young age into a multi-talented artist. He started with dancing advancing towards acting, martial arts, gymnastics, and voice. Later, he also learned guitar, piano, and drum.

Who Are Mystic Inscho Parents?

Regarding his parents, Mystic was born to his mom Jin Li, a programmer, and dad John Inscho, a teacher.

They all live in Los Angeles, California.

Jin Li is from China and Mystic feels proud to have Chinese heritage. She traveled full-time with him during his almost a year-long Broadway tour.

Mystic got into acting as his parents started finding auditions for him and prepared him for the auditions.
