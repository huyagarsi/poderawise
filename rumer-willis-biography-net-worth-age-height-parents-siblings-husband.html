Rumer Willis Biography; Net Worth, Age, Height, Parents, Siblings, Husband

Rumer Glenn Willis is an American actress and singer. She was born on August 16, 1988, at Western Baptist Hospital in Paducah, Kentucky. She is the eldest daughter of actress Demi Moore and actor, Bruce Willis.

Rumer attended Interlochen Arts Academy in Interlochen, Michigan. In January 2004, she enrolled as a sophomore at Wildwood Secondary School in Los Angeles, California and then moved to the University of Southern California for one semester before dropping out.

In 1995, Rumer made her first debut alongside her mother in Now and Then. The following year, she appeared in Striptease and also worked with her father three times in the Whole Nine Yards, Hostage and Air Strike in 2018. Rumer also made guest roles in TV Shows like Miss Guild, Army Wives, CSI: NY and many others.
