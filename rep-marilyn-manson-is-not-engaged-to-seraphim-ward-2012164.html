Rep: Marilyn Manson Is NOT Engaged to Seraphim Ward!

August 05, 2024
blog

No one was more surprised to learn about Marilyn Manson's "engagement" than the rocker himself.

A rep for Seraphim Ward, an aspiring actress and self-described "independent occult filmmaker," told E! News Monday that the two stars are planning to tie the knot. "Seraphim has recently made a life commitment to her partner and couldn't be happier!"

There's just one problem: "This is untrue," Manson's rep tells Us Weekly exclusively. "He has never met this woman."

Ward, 27 — who will make her feature film debut in Speed Dragon, also starring Bai Ling, 45 — even tweeted a photo of her "engagement ring" Saturday with the caption "OMG!" Her rep told E! News the bauble is a family heirloom that "holds very special meaning to both parties."
