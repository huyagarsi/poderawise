Kate Hudson Is 'Almost Brunette' Amid COVID-19 Quarantining: Pic

May 30, 2024
blog

Kate Hudson may be saying goodbye to her signature blonde strands!

On Wednesday, August 19, the 41-year-old actress took to her Instagram Story to show off her overgrown brunette roots after forgoing regular hair appointments when quarantining during the COVID-19 pandemic.

“Looks like I’m almost a brunette…” she wrote over a mirror selfie wearing a seriously cool tie-dye look.

In the next story, she shared an up-close look at her dark roots asking people to vote on whether she should stay “au naturale” or “blond ‘er up!” Though the results are about 50/50, when we checked the majority voted for her to keep it the darker hue.

The Almost Famous star isn’t the only A-lister who’s been embracing their natural hair color during the coronavirus outbreak. Back in March, Teddi Mellencamp shared an Instagram snap of her grown-out dark roots. “Quarantine Day 12 and it looks like my hair and nails have received the memo to social distance themselves from me,” she wrote in the accompanying caption. “After chatting with my girls I realized I am not the only one. Who else out there is with me?”

With that being said, the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star has since colored her hair blonde and even pink.

Then there’s Kelly Ripa, who has been very outspoken from the beginning about her gray roots, sharing updates throughout. Recently, she even made a joke about it!

On Saturday, August 15, the Live With Kelly and Ryan shared a meme that showcased her roots getting grayer each month as part of the #2020Challenge. “Perhaps this meme is a week old at this point … but does that really compare to these roots?” she captioned the post. “My version of the #2020Calendar is just … real.”
