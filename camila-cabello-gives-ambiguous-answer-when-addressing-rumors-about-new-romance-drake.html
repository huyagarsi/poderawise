Camila Cabello's Coy Answer About Rumored Romance With Drake

A few months ago, Camila Cabello was seen out with Drake in Turks and Caicos, and naturally, everyone who found out about it assumed there was love in the air. The two artists were spotted seemingly flirting on a boat, and then jet-skiing together, and fans were floored by the unlikely pairing.

Some fans, however, didn't jump to the conclusion that it was a romantic retreat, and instead speculated that there was a collaboration in the works. Camila Cabello acted mysterious when asked to clarify the purpose of the trip, but she did drop some hints.

She said that the trip was for "work", but the fact that she added a wink and a sip of her drink immediately after made it look suggestive. Of course, they weren't working on the boat, and she did admit that "it was vacation plus more." About Drake specifically, she said "I love that man. I love him... We all love him."

Camila Cabello skillfully avoided having to share anything specific about the rumors, instead saying "Have I ever had what? Like, a beautiful, artistic collaboration with him? I may or may not have."
