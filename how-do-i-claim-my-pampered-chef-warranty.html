What’s the difference between lifetime warranty and lifetime guarantee?

A lifetime warranty is a promise by the manufacturer that the product will be free from defects for the lifetime of the product. However, the “lifetime” of the product is often not defined, and it can vary depending on the manufacturer. For example, a lifetime warranty on a watch might mean the lifetime of the watch movement, which could be 20 years or more. A lifetime warranty on a car might mean the lifetime of the original owner, which could be 10 years or less.
