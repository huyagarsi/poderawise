Sophia Bush Maxim Cover: Wears Sexy Mesh Corset

June 16, 2024

Smokin' Sophia! Chicago PD star Sophia Bush smolders on the cover of Maxim magazine's April 2014 issue wearing nothing but a sexy mesh corset.

The One Tree Hill alum, 31, models a smokey eye and various sexy outfits, including one with handcuffs(!), in the accompanying spread. Bush noted how her onscreen transformation — since playing the independent Brooke Davis on the WB (and later CW) series, which ended after nine seasons in 2012 — was part of her personal journey.

"Well, it's funny. It's not often that someone gets a job at 20 and then does it for a decade," she said of her breakout role on One Tree Hill. "Those are the years you're supposed to be growing and exploring new things…" explained the star, who was briefly married to One Tree Hill costar Chad Michael Murray back in 2005.

"But this show? It feels like the job I was supposed to be doing," Bush added of her butt-kicking character on Chicago PD. On the NBC series, the actress plays Detective Erin Lindsay, a former confidential informant, and like Us, she's done her research! "I did spend my Super Bowl Sunday watching a True Detective marathon on HBO," she told Maxim.

Now in her thirties, the social media-savvy star, with nearly 1 million followers on Twitter, has been using her celebrity to promote causes close to her heart. "I realized I could have a dinner party conversation with 15 people, and I could use Twitter to have that same conversation with a million," the USC grad noted to the mag.
