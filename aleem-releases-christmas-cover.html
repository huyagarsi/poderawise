Aleem Releases Christmas Cover - CelebMix

July 03, 2024
blog

American singer Aleem is officially a star in the making! The rising star is making a name for himself around the world and earlier this year he supported US girlband Fifth Harmony on their European 7/27 tour! He opened for the superstars at huge venues including the prestigious O2 Arena in London.

Now, Aleem is back with his new single, a cover of the Christmas classic ‘Please Come Home For Christmas’. The singer has also released a super festive music video for his version of the Charles Brown hit.

The singer has also recently released his debut EP titled ‘Open Letters‘. The collection features four songs; ‘Inside Out’, ‘Are We Gonna Make It’, ‘Find Out’, and ‘We’ll Get This Right’.
