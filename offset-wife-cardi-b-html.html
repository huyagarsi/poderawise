Offset Expresses Love For Daughter on Instagram on Her 3rd Birthday

June 30, 2024
blog

Offset took to his Instagram to congratulate his younger daughter, Kulture Kiari Cephus, on her third birthday on July 10, 2021. 

The doting dad expressed his love and pride in a video post accompanied by a cute father-daughter dance.

The ‘Bad and Boujee’ rapper was seen enjoying the moment with his younger daughter, the eldest with his wife Cardi B in the song. 

Cardi also celebrated her daughter’s third birthday with an adorable throwback baby portrait of Kulture lounging in the crib when she was just one month old. 

"Forever my little baby My cancer queen," the ‘WAP’ rapper, who is expecting her second child with Offset, wrote alongside the post. 

A few days back, she also posted a monochrome video of Kulture kissing her pregnant belly. In the beautiful film, the child said that she wanted to kiss the baby inside her belly. 

Kulture Is Gearing up to Take on the Role of the Older Sibling

Cardi shared a stunning maternity portrait of herself with Kulture in June 2021. In the picture, she could be seen adoringly stroking her mother's pregnant belly while wearing matching white dresses and gold jewelry in the photo.

She revealed her toddler was preparing to step into the shoes of an older sibling, and she had no doubt Kulture would be a terrific role model to her younger sibling. 

Reminiscing on her own connection with her younger sister, Hennessy Carolina, she commented that since they would be just three years apart, she’s sure they'll love one another a lot and fight a lot just like she and Carolina did.

Offset and Cardi B Announced the Pregnancy News at the 2021 Bet Awards

Offset and his wife, Cardi B disclosed their second pregnancy news on June 27, 2021, while performing together with his group Migos at the BET Awards.

As Cardi joined the hip-hop trio for a song, she flaunted her Dolce & Gabbana black bodysuit with a sheer panel in the midriff that exposed her developing tummy.

The outfit, which also included a matching pair of pants and boots, was exclusively designed by stylist Kollin Carter.

The next day, she also posted a picture of her nude body coated in white paint on Instagram to share the news with her followers. 

Cardi and Offset were romantically linked in early 2017, after being seen spending quality time together at that year's Super Bowl.

In October of the same year, the pair got married in front of an audience at one of her shows. However, it was later found that they had exchanged wedding vows a month before.

The ‘I Like It’ rapper was rumored to be pregnant throughout the first half of 2018, but she didn't address the rumors until her performance on Saturday Night Live in April.

She exposed her baby bump on stage, which was already six weeks at the very time.

Despite the good and bad times in their relationship, including a divorce petition last fall, the couple reconciled, and they are now ready to be parents to two beautiful kids.
