Oscar Filho, born Oscar Francisco de Moraes Junior, embarked on his journey in Atibaia, Brazil. His foray into comedy began in the early 2000s, laying the groundwork for a career that would soon flourish.

The comedian’s breakthrough came with the founding of the Clube da Comédia Stand-up. This initiative marked the inception of stand-up comedy’s popularity in São Paulo, positioning Oscar Filho as a pioneer in the Brazilian comedy scene.
