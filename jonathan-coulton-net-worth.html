Jonathan Coulton, known to the world as JoCo, embarked on his musical journey with a background that was anything but ordinary.

Graduating from Yale University, where he was a member of the prestigious a cappella group the Yale Whiffenpoofs, Coulton initially pursued a career in computer programming.

However, his passion for music and storytelling, combined with his geeky sensibilities, eventually led him down a different path.

From Code to Chords

His transition from coding to chords was marked by his innovative use of the internet to reach an audience. His early career was significantly boosted by his project Thing a Week, where he challenged himself to release a new song every week for a year.
